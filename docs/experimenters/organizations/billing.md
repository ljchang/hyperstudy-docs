# Billing & Plans

HyperStudy offers three plan tiers to meet different research needs. This guide explains plan features, pricing, and how to manage your subscription.

## Plan Comparison

| Feature | Free | Pro | Enterprise |
|---------|------|-----|------------|
| **Experiments** | 3 | 25 | Unlimited |
| **Participants/Month** | 50 | 500 | Unlimited |
| **Storage** | 1 GB | 50 GB | 500 GB |
| **Team Members** | 1 | 5 | Unlimited |
| **Video Chat** | - | 1,000 min/mo | Unlimited |
| **Recording Storage** | - | 25 GB | 100 GB |
| **HLS Transcoding** | - | Yes | Yes |
| **API Access** | - | Yes | Yes |
| **Custom Branding** | - | - | Yes |
| **Priority Support** | - | - | Yes |

## Plan Details

### Free Plan

The Free plan is ideal for:
- Individual researchers getting started
- Small pilot studies
- Testing the platform

**Limitations:**
- No video chat capabilities
- No API access for external integrations
- Single user only

### Pro Plan

The Pro plan is designed for:
- Research teams and labs
- Studies requiring video chat
- Projects needing API integration

**Key Features:**
- **Video Chat** - Real-time audio/video communication between participants
- **HLS Transcoding** - Automatic video optimization for smooth playback
- **API Access** - Programmatic access to your data

### Enterprise Plan

The Enterprise plan is built for:
- University departments
- Research institutions
- Large-scale studies

**Key Features:**
- **Unlimited resources** - No caps on experiments or participants
- **Custom Branding** - Add your institution's logo and colors
- **Priority Support** - Dedicated support channel

## Upgrading Your Plan

To upgrade to a paid plan:

1. Go to **Settings > Organizations**
2. Select your organization
3. Click the **Billing** tab
4. Click **Upgrade Plan**
5. Select your desired plan
6. Enter payment information
7. Confirm the upgrade

Your new plan takes effect immediately.

### What Happens When You Upgrade

- New quotas are available immediately
- All new features are enabled
- Existing experiments continue unchanged
- Billing cycle starts from upgrade date

## Managing Your Subscription

### Viewing Billing Information

The Billing tab shows:
- Current plan and status
- Next billing date
- Payment method
- Billing history

### Updating Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings > Organizations > Billing**
2. Click **Manage Payment Method**
3. Enter new payment details
4. Click **Update**

### Downloading Invoices

1. Go to **Settings > Organizations > Billing**
2. Scroll to **Billing History**
3. Click the download icon next to any invoice

## Changing Plans

### Upgrading

Upgrading takes effect immediately. You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle.

### Downgrading

To downgrade to a lower plan:

1. Ensure your usage is within the new plan's limits
2. Go to **Billing > Change Plan**
3. Select the lower plan
4. Confirm the downgrade

The downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

:::warning
Before downgrading, ensure your:
- Experiment count is within the new limit
- Storage usage is within the new limit
- Team size is within the new limit

You cannot downgrade if you exceed the new plan's quotas.
:::

### Canceling

To cancel your paid subscription:

1. Go to **Settings > Organizations > Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm cancellation

After cancellation:
- Your plan continues until the end of the billing period
- You'll be moved to the Free plan
- Data exceeding Free plan limits may become inaccessible

## Plan Status

Your subscription can have the following statuses:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Active** | Subscription is current and paid |
| **Trialing** | On a free trial period |
| **Past Due** | Payment failed, retrying |
| **Canceled** | Subscription ended |

### Handling Payment Failures

If a payment fails:
1. You'll receive an email notification
2. We'll retry the payment several times
3. Update your payment method if needed
4. After multiple failures, your plan may be downgraded
